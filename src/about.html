Welcome to Namaste Journeys

Your trusted travel companion for exploring the vibrant colors, rich heritage, and breathtaking landscapes of India.

Founded in 2015 by Mr. Arjun Mehra, a passionate traveler and cultural enthusiast, our company was built with a vision to bring authentic travel experiences to everyone.

Our Mission

To offer authentic, safe, and immersive travel experiences that celebrate India’s diversity, culture, and history while promoting sustainable tourism.

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted tourism platform, inspiring travelers to explore every corner of the country with comfort, convenience, and cultural enrichment.

What We Offer

Custom Travel Packages: Tailored tours to suit your budget and interests.
City & Heritage Tours: Explore India’s historical landmarks and UNESCO sites.
Adventure Travel: Trekking, rafting, wildlife safaris, and more.
Cultural Experiences: Food tours, festivals, village stays, and workshops.
Hotel & Transport Booking: Hassle-free travel arrangements.
